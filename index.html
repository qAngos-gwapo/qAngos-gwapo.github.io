What is Gintama?

Gintama is a Japanese manga written and illustrated by a Gorilla Mangaka named Hideaki Sorachi and was turned into an anime by Sunrise from episodes 1-252, and later by Bandai Namco from 253-367.

Gintama: The Final

Last October 10, 2020, during Sakata Gintoki's birthday, the trailer for the final movie, which will be released on January 8 in Japan next year, was released, along with the movie poster. This movie will wrap up the manga, since the anime finished last 2018, when the manga wasn't finished yet.
